# Cat ’Em Up — Creative Context

**Doc ID:** CATEMUP-CONTEXT-CREATIVE  
**Version:** 1.0.0

---

## Project Identity

Title: Cat ’Em Up  
Tagline: Insert Coin. Break Bones. Claws Out.

Genre: Retro 80s Beat ’Em Up  
Platform: Browser-based (initial target)

Cat ’Em Up is a high-energy neon arcade beat ’em up inspired by classic late 80s cabinet games and action movie posters.

This is not parody.  
This is not a meme project.  
This is pure arcade intensity with stylistic confidence.

---

## Tone (Locked)

- Serious arcade action
- Stylish, controlled humor
- No irony overload
- No meme spam
- Confident 80s energy

Visual references:

- Arcade cabinet art
- VHS action movie covers
- Neon urban nights
- Leather jackets and freeze-frames

---

## Visual Identity

- Neon purple / magenta / cyan palette
- Urban alley environments
- Strong silhouettes
- High-contrast lighting
- Dramatic freeze-frames
- Optional subtle CRT flavor

Hero aesthetic:

- Anthropomorphic cats
- Leather jackets
- Worn denim
- Gloves and claws
- 80s action framing

---

## World — Meowami City

A neon-soaked coastal metropolis built by cats and consumed by corruption.

- Industrial docks
- Underground clubs
- Vertical architecture
- Fog + sea air
- Flickering signage
- Art deco + neon

Violence is structure.  
Territory is identity.

Four cats refuse to accept decay.

They are not saving Meowami.

They are taking it back — one block at a time.

---

## Core Team

- Leader — Balance / Control
- Bruiser — Heavy / Grapple
- Blazer — Speed / Pressure
- Rogue — Counter / Precision

Strong silhouettes.  
Distinct combat identity.  
Clear physical hierarchy.

## Music Direction

Style:

- Synthwave accents

Tempo:

128–135 BPM

Rules:

- Instrumental only
- Seamless gameplay loop
- High-energy
- Gameplay-focused

Energy level: relentless arcade drive.

---

## Gameplay Philosophy

Arcade mindset.

- Immediate action
- Clear feedback
- Heavy hit impact
- Minimal fluff
- Tight combat loop

Scope discipline is critical.

---

## Gameplay Scope (Initial)

- 1 polished level
- Side-scrolling progression
- Arena lock segments
- 2–3 enemy types
- 1 boss
- Freeze-frame ending:
  "To Be Continued..."

No feature creep.
