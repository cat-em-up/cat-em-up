## Role

Fast Cat

## Working Name

Jett

## Archetype

Speed / Rhythm / Metal Queen

## Motto

Breaking the Law. Unleash the Claw.

## Physical Build

- Lean, athletic body
- Elongated leg line
- Highly dynamic posture
- Lightweight appearance

## Visual Identity

- Red-orange fur with copper highlights
- Platinum blonde wild 80s hair
- Sharp feline facial structure
- Predatory gaze
- Neon-reflective eyes under stage lighting

## Outfit

- Tight black leather pants
- Sleeveless leather vest with metal studs
- Chains attached to belt (motion accent)
- High black leather boots
- Fingerless gloves
- Dark stage-style eye makeup

## Expression & Presence

- Fierce controlled aggression
- Half-smirk with challenge in the eyes
- Hair constantly in motion
- Dominant forward-leaning stance
- Feels explosive rather than heavy

## Personality

- High energy
- Direct and fearless
- Charismatic
- More expressive than Leader
- Lives in the spotlight, fights like it's a stage

## Combat Identity

- High mobility
- Fast strike chains
- Aerial flow
- Rhythm-driven pressure
- Momentum-based aggression (stronger while advancing)

## Design Notes

- Motion emphasis (hair, chains)
- Strong readable silhouette
- 80s metal queen archetype
- Avoid glam parody exaggeration
- Energy must feel intense, not comedic
