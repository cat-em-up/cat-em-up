# Characters

Working document for core playable characters of **Cat 'Em Up / Meowami**.  
Names are temporary placeholders. Final names will be defined later.

---

## 1. Leader (Working name: Timmy)

### Archetype

Balanced / Charismatic Leader

### Visual Concept

- Anthropomorphic street cat (based on real reference cat)
- Athletic, lean build (clearly smaller than Bruiser)
- Black biker leather jacket (kosuha)
- Full black leather gloves
- Worn jeans
- Large metal belt buckle (motorcycle emblem)
- Toothpick in mouth
- Arms crossed on chest (strong poster silhouette)

### Face & Expression

- Slight head tilt
- Focused look to the side
- Closed-mouth confident smirk ("Flash Before Impact")
- Calm, controlled eyes (not aggressive)

### Personality

- Natural center of gravity of the team
- Speaks little, but clearly
- Light irony, but never clownish
- Moves with confidence, never rushes

### Combat Identity

- Clean, efficient strikes
- Balanced speed and power
- Smooth combo flow
- Poster-hero energy

## 2. Bruiser (Working name: Danny)

### Archetype

Heavy / Grappler / Old School Fighter

### Visual Concept

- Anthropomorphic Maine Coon
- Broad shoulders, thick neck, massive upper frame
- Light gray fur
- Thick old-school masculine mustache (graphic, black)
- Black sleeveless leather vest
- White tank top
- Dark worn jeans
- Black wrist wraps

### Face & Expression

- Calm, unreadable face
- Cold gray eyes
- No exaggerated emotions
- Minimal facial movement

### Personality

- Quiet presence
- Speaks rarely
- Experienced, grounded
- Does not posture, does not brag

### Combat Identity

- Clinch and grappling focus
- Heavy knees and body strikes
- Ground control
- Slow but impactful animations
